Customer type behaviours in Ford GoBike System¶

by Darragh Merrick¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this presentation, I will compare the two types of Customers in the Ford GoBike System Dateset, customers and subscribers, in the following features:

  • Distance travel
  • Trip duration
  • Year of birth

    • Dataset Overview¶

    Ford GoBike System Dateset is a dateset that contains trip data from the bike service for public use. Variables including, trip duration, start time and end time with date, start station and end station names, start and end coordinates, customer type, year of birth and gender.

    Amount of Customers vs Amount of Subscribers¶

    In the dataset, there are more Subscriber than Customers users.

    Distance travel vs Customer type¶

    subscribers are more likey to take short length trips, while customers are more likey to take long length trips.

    note: outliers where removed from this plot.

    Year of birth vs Customer type¶

    They are no significant age difference between the groups.

    note: Year of birth axis starts at 1940.

    Trip duration vs Customer type¶

    Subscribers are more likely to take short trip duration, while customers are more likey to take long trip duration.

    Distance travel vs Year of birth vs Customer type¶

    Subscribers are more likey travel from 500 to 1500m and are between 20 - 30 year old
